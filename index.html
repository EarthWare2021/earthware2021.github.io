About Us

We are a non-profit organization aiming to bring people together during a time in which it is difficult to connect. We teach people how to make masks out of 100% recycled material and have a great time in the process.
Read here for more.

Why?

It is vital for our mental health to maintain connections with others, however the pandemic makes this extremely difficult. That's why we decided to create a course that gets people together virtually while helping the environment in the process.
In addition, there is a severe pollution problem in our oceans. There are currently more disposable masks than jellyfish in the ocean. We plan to use old cloth from your own wardrobe to produce new, safe masks that all can enjoy.
