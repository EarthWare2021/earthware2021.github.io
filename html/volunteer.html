Click here for the link to the sign up form!


How it works:

Once you sign up, we provide you with the training you need during a zoom call with other volunteers around the globe. Using old clothing, you will make a reusable mask!

When you have completed the sign up sheet on our Volunteer page, you will be sent a zoom link via email. This zoom will take place during one of your listed available times. If you have prior experience with making homemade masks, the zoom training is not required, but it is an amazing opportunity to connect with others who are passionate about the environment-- just like you!

If you do not have one of the necessary items on the sign up sheet, we will drop it off at the address you have listed. If you have all of your materials, you are all set to create a mask! After completing your homemade mask, you may either:

  1. Send it back to us to sell, where all proceeds are funneled back into our program if you are interested in this option, please visit our donate page for the drop off details.
  2. Keep it and rock your new mask yourself!

Interested in volunteer credit?

No problem! After completing your zoom training session, we will send you a certificate of volunteer participation. If you need us to write a letter for proof, contact one of our team members, and they will be happy to write one.


Thank you for your support!
