Earth Ware - Our Mission

Protect yourself, and your planet.

We train you to create eco-friendly
masks and help you socialize with
others during an unsettled time.

Training is fully virtual giving
volunteers around the world access
to our classes. These sessions are
held on multiple days compatible w
ith all time zones for your convenience!.


We also incorporate fun activities for the
volunteers to bond that will ultimately
help them in the mask making process.
This gives our volunteers motivation
for making masks, but also a fun experience.
